First Project

Our First Project was pretty basic.My first project was on Code.org where I learned the basics of creating a minecraft game. The coding was a drag and drop. So we had different commands on the right side of the screen, and a empty box on the left side where we dragged commands like move forward, or turn left or right to make the player move. There was a total of 14 levels and once you got to 14 you had to create your own.
